**Know-MRI**, our knowledge-mechanism revealer & interpreter for LLMs, was released as an ACL 2025 System Demonstrations paper and open-sourced at [github.com/nlpkeg/Know-MRI](https://github.com/nlpkeg/Know-MRI) (MIT). It offers a one-stop interface to probe what a model knows and where it lives.
