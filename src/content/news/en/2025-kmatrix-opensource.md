**KMatrix**, a heterogeneous knowledge collaborative-enhancement toolkit for LLMs, is open-sourced at [github.com/nlpkeg/KMatrix](https://github.com/nlpkeg/KMatrix) (Apache-2.0), followed by the KMatrix-2 and KMatrix-CR variants for attributable knowledge-augmented generation.
